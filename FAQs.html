#Frequent Asked Questions

            1. When is fees due?

            Full school fees is due on the first day of the semester. 

            2. How can I get financial Clearance?

            After payment of fees, you can visit the Finance office at the student accounts counter for financial clearance. You can also email studentaccounts@kajiadowesttechnical.ac.ke

            3. How do I get my holds removed?

            After payment of fees, the system should automatically remove the financial hold on your account. In case of any issues, you can visit the Finance office at the student accounts counter for financial clearance. You can also email: studentaccounts@kajiadowesttechnical.ac.ke

            4. Is there a difference between holds and Financial Clearance?

            Yes, the holds are put for different reasons e.g. Students with balances and are not on a payment plan. Financial Clearance is given when you satisfy all the finance requirements to attend classes. 

            5. When can I get clearance to attend classes?

            Financial clearance is given to registered students from the beginning of the semester up to the end of the 2nd week of the semester which is the deadline of your financial clearance to attend classes and when payments are made during the semester. 

            6. Can I pay my fees in instalments?

            Yes, a deferred payment plan is available for continuing students, where fees is paid in 3 equal instalments. The first payment is due by the 2nd week of school. Students who wish to be on this plan should contact the student accounts for more information.
            7. Can I go for school trips if my school fees is not cleared?

            No, only students with financial clearance are allowed to go for any school trip. If any funds are paid to the trip account and the student is not cleared, the money will be used to clear the outstanding school fees. 

            8. Can I do my exams if am not financially cleared?

            No, only financially cleared student will be allowed to do exams.
            9. When can I get clearance to attend classes?

            Financial clearance is given to registered students who have cleared their school fees up to the last day of financial clearance as marked in the academic calendar. 

            10. I made a payment and it is not reflecting in my account?

            Kindly visit the finance office and request to talk to the finance officer for assistance.

            11. I’m unable to clear my fees on time, how can you assist me? What options do I have?

            Visit the Registrar’s office for more information. You can also email: registrar@kajiadowesttechnical.ac.ke

            12. How much is the fees for next semester?

            Check the fees structure OR visit the student accounts office or you can also email studentaccounts@kajiadowesttechnical.ac.ke

            13. Do I get charged for dropping /withdrawing classes?

            Yes, withdrawn class fee charges begin from the day the semester starts. Courses withdrawn within the 1st week of semester attracts a penalty of 10% of the tuition, Courses withdrawn within the 2nd week of the semester will attract a penalty of 25% of tuition .After the second week the charge will be 100% of the tuition. 

            14. Do you work over the weekends?

            The finance office is open on the first two Saturdays of the semester from 9.00am to 1.00pm

            15. How long will it take a bank deposit or MPESA payment to reflect in my account?

            Your bank cash deposit or MPESA payment will be updated in your account within 24 hours

            16. Do I have to present my bank deposit slip at the cashiers?

            Yes, you do. If not the amount shall be receipted from our bank statements the following week.

            17. How do I get a refund of my caution money?

            After final clearance, a refund form can be collected from the finance office or downloaded from the website. Once filled in, it can be dropped at the finance office or scanned and forwarded to studentaccounts@kajiadowesttechnical.ac.ke.
